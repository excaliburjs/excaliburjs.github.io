Enumeration CollisionType

An enum that describes the types of collisions actors can participate in

Enumeration members

Active

Active:
  • Defined in Actor.ts:1109

Actors with the Active setting raise collision events and participate in collisions with other actors and will be push or moved by actors sharing the Active or Fixed setting.

Elastic

Elastic:
  • Defined in Actor.ts:1116

Actors with the Elastic setting will behave the same as Active, except that they will "bounce" in the opposite direction given their velocity dx/dy. This is a naive implementation meant for prototyping, for a more robust elastic collision listen to the "collision" event and perform your custom logic.

obsolete

This behavior will be handled by a future physics system

Fixed

Fixed:
  • Defined in Actor.ts:1125

Actors with the Fixed setting raise collision events and participate in collisions with other actors. Actors with the Fixed setting will not be pushed or moved by other actors sharing the Fixed. Think of Fixed actors as "immovable/onstoppable" objects. If two Fixed actors meet they will not be pushed or moved by each other, they will not interact except to throw collision events.

Passive

Passive:
  • Defined in Actor.ts:1103

Actors with the Passive setting only raise collision events, but are not influenced or moved by other actors and do not influence or move other actors.

PreventCollision

PreventCollision:
  • Defined in Actor.ts:1098

Actors with the PreventCollision setting do not participate in any collisions and do not raise collision events.
